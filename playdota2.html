Instructions

Chrome

Just click here and and click continue and install.
Don't worry about the message. It just means that the userscript knows what website you are on because it needs that to check whether you are on www.playdota.com or not.
This userscript does not store any of your past web history usage.

Firefox

  1. Download Greasemonkey
  2. Click here and install the userscript.

Also available as a bookmarklet

* if you prefer being able to switch between DotA and Dota 2 icons
  1. Open Bookmarks Bar (Ctrl+Shift+B for Chrome)
  2. Drag the following bookmarklet to the Bookmarks Bar

    PlayDota2

  3. Visit playdota.com and click the bookmarklet to use it.
  4. Hit Refresh to revert back to original DotA icons.
